Privacy Policy for Dock Manager

Last updated: 26 Sep, 2025

Dock Manager is developed by RCHelpApps. This app helps manage dock truck schedules and runs completely on your device.

Data Collection

Dock Manager does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All truck names, timers, and history are stored only on your device and are never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

Dock Manager does not use analytics, ads, or any third-party SDKs that track users.

Children’s Privacy

Since we do not collect any personal information, the app is safe for all ages.
